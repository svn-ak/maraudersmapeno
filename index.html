The Prefect's Guide to Outsmarting Muggles

Small London spells that make the city less confusing in week one.

Maps Charm

Citymapper before chaos.

For London transit, Citymapper is often clearer than generic maps. It tells you platform hints, carriage advice, and better exits.

Escalator Law

Stand right. Walk left.

On Tube escalators, standing on the left during rush hour is how you accidentally start a small war.

Oxford Circus Trap

Some Tube rides are fake shortcuts.

One-stop central-London Tube journeys can take longer than walking above ground. Check the walking time first.

Bus Hopper Spell

One tap, many buses.

London's bus Hopper fare lets you take unlimited bus/tram journeys within an hour of your first tap.

Plastic Galleon

Contactless is normal.

Use a contactless card or phone on the yellow readers. Cash will mostly sit there like a decorative prop.
